*Submission Instructions*

#The _Journal of Alchemical Engineering_ is a collection of user-created puzzles curated by Zachtronics. To submit a puzzle for inclusion in the _Journal_, upload your puzzle to Steam Workshop and send the following information to *zach@zachtronics.com*:
 
• a link to your puzzle on Steam Workshop,
• an explanation of what makes your puzzle interesting,
• an explanation of what you imagine your puzzle's product(s) to be, and
• the name you would like attached to the puzzle, in the format ""Last, F."" (if your full name were ""First Last"").

#*Submission Rules*

#By submitting a puzzle for inclusion in the _Journal of Alchemical Engineering_, you are granting us full permission with no limitations to modify and distribute that puzzle as part of the game. You will receive no monetary compensation for your submission; our consideration of your puzzle and credit attribution in a manner described above shall be your sole and full compensation.

You may only submit puzzles that you, yourself, have created.

You agree to be bound by the terms of the Steam® Subscriber Agreement.

Do not submit the same puzzle more than once.

#*Puzzle Design Tips*

#Aim to create something that is both uniquely interesting and consistent with the game's setting and aesthetics. For example, just because you can use every type of atom in a single product or reagent doesn't mean you should. The best puzzles are engaging in their own right _and_ evocative of the world of the game.
